About Me

Follow @wdeb

I am a psychologist turned designer from Leiden, The Netherlands. I love helping software understand humans by crafting simple and friendly interfaces. I am a co-founder and designer at Gibbon, the easiest platform to learn from each other.

I believe that creating pretty pixels is not enough, making software easy to use and seemingly simple is the real challenge. There is no silver bullet for designing perfect user experiences. It is hard work, sweat and determination that makes great products & experiences.

My designs would be useless pretty pixels if it wasn't for my partners in crime Joeri Djojosoeparto, Petar Radoševiç and Wout Laban who are the best programmers & growth hacker in the world.
